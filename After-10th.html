After completing 10th grade, students can choose streams or vocational paths based on their interest and career goals. Here are some options:

Science Stream (10+2)

Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics.

Career Paths: Engineering, Medical, Research, Architecture, Data Science.

Commerce Stream (10+2)

Subjects: Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, Mathematics.

Career Paths: CA, CMA, Banking, Finance, Entrepreneurship, Business Analytics.

Arts / Humanities Stream (10+2)

Subjects: History, Political Science, Sociology, Psychology, Languages.

Career Paths: Teaching, Civil Services, Journalism, Social Work, Law, Design.

Vocational Courses

Options: ITI courses, Diploma in Engineering, Animation, Fashion Designing, Hospitality, Agriculture, Retail Management, Computer Hardware.

Career Paths: Technician, Animator, Fashion Designer, Hotel Manager, Agricultural Expert, Retail Specialist.

Skill-Based Courses

Options: Web Development, Graphic Designing, Digital Marketing, Coding Bootcamps, Robotics, App Development.

Career Paths: Freelancer, IT Professional, Designer, Entrepreneur.

International / Competitive Pathways

Options: Foundation courses for NEET, JEE, Olympiads, or studying abroad preparation courses.

Career Paths: Doctor, Engineer, Researcher, Global Career Opportunities.
